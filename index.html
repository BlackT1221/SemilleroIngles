About us

Vision

We envision a leading online platform for SENA trainees to strengthen their English vocabulary in table and bar settings, renowned for excellence in delivering specialized educational content and interactive tools that make learning accessible and effective. We strive to be an essential resource for hospitality professionals on their journey to English fluency, contributing to their success and growth in a global marketplace.

Mision

To provide SENA table and bar trainees with tools and resources to improve their English vocabulary effectively and practically. We provide a dynamic and friendly online environment that encourages continuous learning and English proficiency, helping them advance their hospitality careers.

Main objective

Create an interactive online platform for students to generate and share educational resources, including technical vocabulary, to promote active learning and high-quality materials in the mesa and bar program.
